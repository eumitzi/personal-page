The 1975

The 1975 is an English rock band originating from Manchester

Matty Healy picked up music when the best friend of his dad died, leaving him two guitars. His confrontational attitude resulted in getting kicked out of private school at the age of fourteen and started drumming in a punk band with eventual band mates Adam Hann (guitar) and Ross MacDonald (bass) .
